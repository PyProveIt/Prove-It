_neg_domain_in_full_domain:
(see dependencies)

The basic approach is to show
(1) $\ell\in\{2^{t-1}+1\ldots-(\epsilon+1)\}$ means $-2^{t-1}+1 \le \ell$ (and $\ell\in\mathbb{Z}$)
(2) $\ell\in\{2^{t-1}+1\ldots-(\epsilon+1)\}$ means $\ell \le -(\epsilon+1)$
(3) $\epsilon\in\mathbb{N}^{+}$ means $-(\epsilon+1) < 2^{t-1}$
(4) Thus $-2^{t-1 + 1} \le \ell \le 2^{t-1}$ and $\ell$ is an Integer.
(5) Thus $\ell\in\{-2^{t-1 + 1}\ldots 2^{t-1}\}$
