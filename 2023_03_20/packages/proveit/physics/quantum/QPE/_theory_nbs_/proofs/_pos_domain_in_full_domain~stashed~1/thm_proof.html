Under these presumptions, we begin our proof of
_pos_domain_in_full_domain:
(see dependencies)

The basic approach is to show
(1) $\ell\in\{\epsilon+1\ldots 2^{t-1}\}$ means $\ell \le 2^{t-1}$ (and $\ell\in\mathbb{Z}$)
(2) $\ell\in\{\epsilon+1\ldots 2^{t-1}\}$ means $\epsilon+1 \le \ell$
(3) $\epsilon\in\mathbb{N}^{+}$ means $-2^{t-1 + 1} < \epsilon+1$
(4) Thus $-2^{t-1 + 1} \le \ell \le 2^{t-1}$ and $\ell$ is an Integer.
(5) Thus $\ell\in\{-2^{t-1 + 1}\ldots 2^{t-1}\}$
