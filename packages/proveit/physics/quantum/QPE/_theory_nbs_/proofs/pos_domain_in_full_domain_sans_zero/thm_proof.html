pos_domain_in_full_domain_sans_zero:
(see dependencies)

The basic approach is to show that
$\ell\in\{\epsilon+1\ldots 2^{t-1}\}$ means $\ell\in\{-2^{t-1}+1\ldots 2^{t-1}\}$ and $\ell\ne 0$
More specifically:
(1) $\ell\in\{\epsilon+1\ldots 2^{t-1}\}$ means $\ell \le 2^{t-1}$ (and $\ell\in\mathbb{Z}$) (both auto side-effect)
(2) $\ell\in\{\epsilon+1\ldots 2^{t-1}\}$ means $\epsilon+1 \le \ell$ (auto side-effect)
(3) $\epsilon\in\{1\ldots 2^{t-1}-2\}$ means $\epsilon + 1 > 0$
(4) Thus $-2^{t-1 + 1} \le \ell \le 2^{t-1}$ and $\ell>0$.
(5) Thus $-2^{t-1 + 1} \le \ell \le 2^{t-1}$ and $\ell\ne 0$.
(6) Thus $\ell\in\{-2^{t-1 + 1}\ldots 2^{t-1}\}-\{0\}$
